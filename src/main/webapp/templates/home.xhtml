Our Products

Third Party

Third-party offers coverage against claims of damages and losses incurred by a driver who is not the insured, the principal, and is therefore not covered under the insurance policy. The driver who caused damages is the third party.

Comprehensive

Comprehensive Motor Insurance policy covers losses by own damages and third party liability. Comprehensive Motor Insurance package provides you with all the necessary coverage for you and your vehicle.

Fired

Comprehensive motor insurance coverage will cover fire damage to your vehicle regardless of what causes the fire, but it's important that you purchase the coverage for your car insurance policy before the loss occurs.
